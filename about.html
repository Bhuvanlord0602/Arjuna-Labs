About Us

Building India's Most Accessible Prototyping Infrastructure

Our Story

Arjuna Innovation Labs was founded to bridge the gap between theoretical education and real-world hardware development. Students, startups, and independent innovators often face high equipment costs, limited access to tools, and long turnaround times for prototyping.

Our mission is to democratize access to professional-grade fabrication infrastructure, helping innovators transform ideas into tangible products efficiently and affordably.

What We Offer

An integrated ecosystem combining 3D printing, mechanical fabrication, laser cutting, and electronics development under one professionally managed facility. Whether you are building your first robot, developing an IoT product, or creating custom enclosures, we provide the tools, space, and support you need.

Why Bangalore

We launched in Bangalore because of its strong startup, electronics, and manufacturing ecosystem. The city has a vibrant maker culture and a growing hardware innovation community, making it the ideal location for our first facility.

Our Vision

To become India's leading public prototyping infrastructure platform, expanding to multiple cities and enabling thousands of innovators to bring hardware ideas to life.
