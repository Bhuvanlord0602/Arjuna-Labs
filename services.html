3D Printing & Rapid Prototyping

Professional 3D printing services for functional prototypes, product enclosures, and custom components. We offer both FDM and resin printing technologies for various applications.

Services Include

  • Functional prototype development
  • Product enclosure manufacturing
  • Mechanical part prototyping
  • Custom component fabrication
  • Small-batch production runs
  • Design consultation and optimization

Technologies

  • FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)
  • SLA / Resin Printing
  • Materials: PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, Flexible TPU
  • Layer resolution: 50-300 microns
  • Build volume: up to 300x300x400mm

Pricing: Pay-per-gram model with bulk discounts and member benefits.

Mechanical Fabrication & Laser Cutting

Complete mechanical prototyping and fabrication services including precision laser cutting, bench drilling, and light CNC operations for metal and non-metal materials.

Services Include

  • Precision laser cutting (wood, acrylic, MDF, cardboard)
  • Bench drilling and assembly
  • Metal and plastic fabrication
  • Light CNC prototyping
  • Custom jig and fixture creation
  • Material cutting and shaping

Equipment & Materials

  • CO2 Laser Cutter (600x400mm cutting area)
  • Bench drilling machines and hand tools
  • Safety equipment and workbenches
  • Acrylic up to 10mm, Wood/MDF up to 12mm
  • Cardboard, paper, aluminum and thin metals

Pricing: Hourly machine rates, per-project quotations, and material cost options.

Access Models & Safety

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to use the facility?

No. Beginners are welcome. We provide onboarding, safety guidance, and tool orientation.

How do equipment bookings work?

Members get priority booking slots. Non-members can reserve based on availability and project requirements.

Do you provide materials or should I bring my own?

Both options are available. In-house stock is available for common materials, and customer-supplied material can be approved after checks.

Can startups get dedicated project support?

Yes. We offer custom corporate/startup plans with bulk usage, priority support, and technical consultations.
